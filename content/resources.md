## Job opportunities

Applications are now open for the [PhD in Environmental Studies](https://gsas.nyu.edu/) at NYU's Graduate School of Arts & Science. **The application deadline is December 1.** Follow these links for [FAQs](https://as.nyu.edu/departments/environment.html) and details on the application process and requirements.

### New PhD/JD in Environmental Studies

A dual-degree program between NYU's School of Law and the Environmental Studies Department is now available for prospective students interested in pursuing a path in law. Please review details on the department website and on the School of Law's dedicated webpage.

### Inquiries

Postdoc and research-assistant openings are listed on the [home page](/) when available; we welcome inquiries even when no positions are listed.
